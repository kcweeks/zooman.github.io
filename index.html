Hello,

My name is K.C. Weeks and welcome to Zooman Studio. I built this site to highlight some of my work and to get my name out there in the graphics and web circles.

I have been a animal care professional for 16 years. In 2009 I had an epiphany. Granted, this epiphany was brought on by a horrible, two donkey accident and two weeks off from work to rest a severely sprained shoulder. I realized that I was not bullet proof and that I was going to need something to fall back if I could not do the zoo thing any more. I quizzed my friends and family as to what I should do and the same answer kept coming up; Digital Media. Graphic design, web programming, and video games, oh my! So, in 2010, I went back to school. I had a little taste of digital media and I wanted more. At that point, I decided on a two year plan. I would get my education and then step out into the graphics and web world with experience and a portfolio at my back. I started working on some sites and studying programming code in my spare time. Then life happened. In the spring of 2011, a budgetary lay off left me on the outside of one industry not sure if I wanted to get back in and on the outside of another that I wanted to be in, but didn't have the experience. 2012 saw a new start. I'm currently a ranch hand for Lazy 5 Ranch while going back to school to get my degree in internet technology. So, here I am. I hope you like what you see and if you have any questions or comments, feel free to contact me at kweeks76@gmail.com.
